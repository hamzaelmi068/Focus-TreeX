# Focus Tree 🌳
![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/2a6774ac-7ce9-4bd0-85ac-525ed5327abd)![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/950f9db3-0156-4f1b-9bb0-346120319b60)


Focus Tree is a unique productivity app that turns your focus time into a beautiful, growing tree. As you maintain concentration, your tree flourishes – creating a visual, rewarding experience that encourages sustained focus and productivity.

## ✨ Features

- **Living Timer** - Watch your tree grow in real-time as you focus
- **Streak System** - Build and maintain daily focus streaks
- **AI Coach** - Receive personalized motivation messages
- **Tree Collection** - Unlock different tree styles as you progress
- **Dark/Light Modes** - Comfortable viewing in any environment

## 🌱 How It Works

![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/3eeb49d1-bcb4-4c1c-8d1c-7e25db47ebb9)

1. **Start Timer** - Begin your focus session (default 25 minutes)
2. **Stay Focused** - Watch your tree grow as time progresses
3. **Complete Session** - Earn rewards and grow your streak
4. **Track Progress** - View your focus statistics and achievements
